## GET
curl -X GET http://localhost:5000/alunos

## POST
curl -X POST http://localhost:5000/alunos -H "Content-Type: application/json" -d "{\"nome\": \"Carla\", \"curso\": \"Ciência da Computação\"}"

## UPDATE
curl -X PUT http://localhost:5000/alunos/1 -H "Content-Type: application/json" -d "{\"curso\": \"Engenharia de Software\"}"

## DELETE
curl -X DELETE http://localhost:5000/alunos/2
